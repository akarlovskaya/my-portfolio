ABOUT ME

Hello!

My name is Anna, I’m web designer based in Vancouver.

My journey in web design and front-end development began in December 2015 from working on my own HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Before that I worked as an account manager in a staffing agency and have gained extensive experience in customer service.

Driven by curiosity and eagerness to learn something new, I dove into web development and realized the beauty of coding and the power of web design principles. I am very passionate about typography and UX design.

If you have any questions or just want to say me “Hello!” — email me or find in socials. I will be happy to talk with you!
